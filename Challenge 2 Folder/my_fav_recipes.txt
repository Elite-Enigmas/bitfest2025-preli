# Recipe 1
Name: Chocolate Cake
Ingredients: Flour: 2 cups, Sugar: 1.5 cups, Cocoa Powder: 0.5 cups, Eggs: 2 large, Butter: 0.5 cups, Baking Powder: 1 tsp
Taste: Sweet
Cuisine: American
Preparation Time: 60
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mix dry ingredients together.
3. Add eggs and butter, and mix well.
4. Pour batter into a greased pan and bake for 30 minutes.

# Recipe 2
Name: Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients: Spaghetti: 200 grams, Eggs: 2 large, Parmesan Cheese: 50 grams, Bacon: 100 grams, Black Pepper: 1 tsp
Taste: Savory
Cuisine: Italian
Preparation Time: 30
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. Fry bacon until crispy.
3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
4. Combine spaghetti, bacon, and egg mixture, and serve.

# Recipe 3
Name: Vegetable Stir Fry
Ingredients: Broccoli: 1 head, Bell Peppers: 2 medium, Carrots: 2 medium, Soy Sauce: 2 tbsp, Garlic: 3 cloves, Ginger: 1 inch piece, grated
Taste: Savory
Cuisine: Asian
Preparation Time: 20
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a wok.
2. Add garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 1 minute.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
4. Add soy sauce, cook for another 2 minutes, and serve.

# Recipe 4
Name: Mango Smoothie
Ingredients: Mango: 2 medium, Yogurt: 1 cup, Milk: 0.5 cups, Honey: 2 tbsp
Taste: Sweet
Cuisine: Fusion
Preparation Time: 10
Instructions:
1. Peel and chop mangoes.
2. Blend mango, yogurt, milk, and honey until smooth.
3. Serve chilled.
