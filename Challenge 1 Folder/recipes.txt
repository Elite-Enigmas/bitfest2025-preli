
Name: Chocolate Cake
Ingredients: Flour, Sugar, Cocoa Powder, Eggs, Butter, Baking Powder
Taste: Sweet
Cuisine: American
Preparation Time: 60
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mix dry ingredients together.
3. Add eggs and butter, and mix well.
4. Pour batter into a greased pan and bake for 30 minutes.

Name: Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients: Spaghetti, Eggs, Parmesan Cheese, Bacon, Black Pepper
Taste: Savory
Cuisine: Italian
Preparation Time: 30
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. Fry bacon until crispy.
3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
4. Combine spaghetti, bacon, and egg mixture, and serve.

Name: Vegetable Stir Fry
Ingredients: Broccoli, Bell Peppers, Carrots, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Ginger
Taste: Savory
Cuisine: Asian
Preparation Time: 20
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a wok.
2. Add garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 1 minute.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
4. Add soy sauce, cook for another 2 minutes, and serve.

Name: Mango Smoothie
Ingredients: Mango, Yogurt, Milk, Honey
Taste: Sweet
Cuisine: Fusion
Preparation Time: 10
Instructions:
1. Peel and chop mangoes.
2. Blend mango, yogurt, milk, and honey until smooth.
3. Serve chilled.
